summed leaf node equals 1:

\beq
\begin{array}{c}
\bcen
\xymatrix{
\sum a
&
\\
y\ar[u]
& x\ar[ul]\ar[l]
}
\ecen
=\xymatrix{y&x\ar[l]}
\\
\sum_a P(a|x, y)P(y|x)=P(y|x)
\end{array}
\eeq
